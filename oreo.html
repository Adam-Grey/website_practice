Ingredients Required:

  • 4-6 Oreo cookies
  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup milk (adjust for desired thickness)
  • Whipped cream (for topping, optional)
  • Extra crushed Oreos (for garnish, optional)

Steps to prepare:

  • Prepare Ingredients: Gather all your ingredients and measure them out.
  • Blend the Cookies: Place the Oreo cookies in a blender. You can break them into smaller pieces for easier blending.
  • Add Ice Cream: Add the vanilla ice cream to the blender with the cookies.
  • Pour in Milk: Add the milk. Start with 1 cup, and you can adjust later for your preferred thickness.
  • Blend: Blend on high until smooth. If the shake is too thick, add more milk a little at a time and blend again.
  • Taste and Adjust: Taste the shake and add more cookies or ice cream if you want a stronger flavor or thicker consistency.
  • Serve: Pour the shake into tall glasses.
  • Add Toppings: Top with whipped cream and sprinkle crushed Oreos on top, if desired.
  • Enjoy: Serve immediately with a straw and enjoy your delicious Oreo shake!

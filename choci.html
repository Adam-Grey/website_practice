Ingredients Required:

  • 2 cups chocolate ice cream
  • 1 cup milk (adjust for desired thickness)
  • 1-2 tablespoons chocolate syrup (optional)
  • Whipped cream (for topping, optional)
  • Chocolate shavings or sprinkles (for garnish, optional)

Steps to prepare:

  • Prepare Ingredients: Gather all your ingredients and measure them out.
  • Add Ice Cream: Place the chocolate ice cream into a blender.
  • Pour in Milk: Add the milk to the blender. Start with 1 cup, adjusting later for your preferred thickness.
  • Add Chocolate Syrup: If you like a richer chocolate flavor, add 1-2 tablespoons of chocolate syrup.
  • Blend: Blend on high until smooth and creamy. If itâ€™s too thick, add more milk gradually and blend again.
  • Taste and Adjust: Taste the shake and add more chocolate syrup if desired for extra sweetness.
  • Serve: Pour the shake into tall glasses.
  • Add Toppings: Top with whipped cream and sprinkle chocolate shavings or sprinkles on top, if desired.
  • Enjoy: Serve immediately with a straw and savor your delicious chocolate ice cream shake!
